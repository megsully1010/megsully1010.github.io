Background on Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is a prominent singer loved by fans all over the world. Swift was born on December 13, 1989 in West Reading, PA. She realeased her first album when she was 16 and now she has released ten albums

Her music has lots of variety and ranges from country,pop, and alternative.

Eras Tour

Currently Taylor Swift is on tour. The tour is known as the "Eras Tour" and contains songs from all ten of her albums. The tour has quickly become on of the higgest grossing tours in US history. In this tour Taylor Swift performs for over 3 hours.
