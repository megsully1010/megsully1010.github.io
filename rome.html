Rome

Why I love Rome

I studied abroad in Rome in the fall of 2021. To say it was an amazing experience is just an understatement. Rome was actually the first european city I ever visted back in 2019. I fell in love the moment I stepped foot there.

Must see spots in Rome

  • The Pantheon
  • The Colosseum
  • The Vatican
  • The Trevi Fountain
  • St. Peter's Basillica
  • Piazza Navona
  • The Spanish Steps
  • Domus Arena
  • Baths of Caracalla

Besides Rome's amazing architecture the city is also known for some of the best cuisine in Italy.

The best Roman cuisine

  • Carbonara
  • Cacio e Pepe
  • Suppli
  • Porchetta
  • Pasta alla gricia
  • Saltimbocca
  • Puntarelle

The Roman cuisine was truly delicous. My personal favorite dishes are carbonara and cacio e pepe.
